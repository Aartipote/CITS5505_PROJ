Learning Module - Symptoms

Watch for Symptoms...

There has been a wide range of symptoms that were reported by the people who had COVID-19. They ranged from mild symptoms to severe illness.

Symptoms appear from the 2-14 days after the exposure to the virus. People having the symptoms listed below may have COVID:

  • Fever or chills
  • Cough
  • Shortness of breath or diffculty in breathing.
  • Fatigue
  • Muscle or body aches
  • Headache
  • New loss of taste or smell
  • Sore throat
  • Congestion or runny nose
  • Nausea or vomiting
  • Diarrhea

NOTE: This list does not include all the symptoms. The list keeps getting updated by the CDC as we learn more about the virus.
