Learning Module - Symptoms

Watch for Symptoms...

After being exposed to Covid-19 it takes approximately 5-6 days to show the symptoms and can stay for around 14 days. People exposed to this are hence advised to stay at home and quarantine so that the spread of the disease can be prevented.

The disease affects people in different ways. Most people who are affected will develop mild to moderate illness and recover without hospitalization.

The most common symptoms are fever, dry cough and tiredness. The symptoms that are not that common are:

  • Aches and pains
  • Sore throat
  • Diarrhoea
  • Conjunctivitis
  • Headache
  • Loss of taste or smell
  • Rash on skin or discolouration of finger or toes

Some people are affected seriously and they undergo:

  • Difficulty in breathing or shortness of breath
  • Chest pain or pressure
  • Loss of speech or movement

NOTE: People of all ages who experience fever and/or cough associated with difficulty breathing or shortness of breath, chest pain or pressure, or loss of speech or movement should seek medical care immediately.
